Uni Projects

These are projects made as assignments for several of my USAL courses. Some of them may need some polish, but were helpful development experiences:

  • SSOOII-Batracios: Process synchronization through IPC processes with UNIX libraries.
    • SSOII-Ranas: Port of the previous project to Windows and WINAPI
  • RedesI-NNTP: Implements a reduced version of the Network News Transport Protocol in pure C sockets, allowing for users to share news and create bulletin boards.
  • PIII-Musicfy: Music management library to learn about OOP and MVC in Java.
  • IGU-CalorieTracker: Calorie tracking software made to learn UI design and development in C# and WPF.
